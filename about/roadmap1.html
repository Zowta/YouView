The Roadmap

Concept and reason

"TGIs is over-rated ... like hugely over-rated"-Daniel Best

Aiming to provide a smooth viewing experience this update looks to be able to allow you to switch between your friends streams. Has your big bis friend just jumped on? Time to stop watching the low tier friend and get some of that main friend content.

Simple concept, buttons for each streaming window to allow you to allocate what stream you wish to replace and enter a new channel id to view.
